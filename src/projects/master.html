Digital Adaptation of Iznik Tile Motifs

April 2024 - October 2024
Master's Project

My thesis explores the digital reinterpretation of Iznik tile motifs, a cornerstone of Istanbul's visual identity, through parametric modeling and Artificial Intelligence. Inspired by the dynamic evolution of cultural heritage, the research questions whether preservation must mean mere replication or if adaptive methods can revitalise cultural memory for the contemporary era. Through expert interviews and experiments with younger audiences, the study emphasises the potential of digital tools to reinterpret traditional motifs, allowing for accessibility and engagement. Positioned at the intersection of art, technology, and heritage, it offers fresh perspectives for the sustainability of cultural identity in a globalised world.
